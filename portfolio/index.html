I am a junior software developer with a strong foundation in web development and a passion for learning and growth in the tech industry. I hold a DTS in software development and have completed a 5-month internship where I worked with technologies such as Java, Spring, and Angular as part of the Jobin Tech program at the International University of Rabat. I have hands-on experience developing Angular applications, utilizing best practices like component-driven architecture, RxJS for reactive programming, and Firebase for backend services and authentication. In addition, I am familiar with SCSS for styling and have explored modern JavaScript frameworks such as React. I am passionate about technology, responsible, and enjoy working in a team environment. With strong interpersonal skills and a commitment to delivering quality solutions, I aim to continue improving my technical abilities and contribute effectively to development projects.
